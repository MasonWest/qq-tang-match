?? Design Highlights
Playful Color Palette: I used a background gradient of Indigo, Purple, and Pink to mimic the "energy" of casual gaming.

Glassmorphism: The header and smaller UI elements use semi-transparent white backgrounds with a blur effect (backdrop-blur-md), giving it a modern, high-end feel.

Micro-interactions:

Framer Motion: Used for the player cards (AnimatePresence) so that when a new player joins or a new card is added, they slide into place smoothly rather than jumping.

States: Buttons have active:scale-95 to mimic a physical button press on mobile.

Responsive Grid:

Desktop: The form stays on the left (4 columns) while the lobby takes up the right (8 columns).

Mobile: The layout stacks vertically, putting the "Action" (the form) at the top.
